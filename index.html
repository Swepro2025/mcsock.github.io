About Us & Our Story

At Yiwu Muchen Knitting Co., Ltd, we are more than just a socks factory — we are innovators in knitting technology and passionate creators of high-quality socks for global brands, retailers, and wholesalers. Established in 2017 and headquartered in China Zhuji(The China's largest hosiery capital), we specialize in crafting custom-designed socks, private label socks, and OEM/ ODM services that meet diverse market demands.

Feel free to reach out to us if you have any inquiries or uncertainties regarding our services. We are here to assist you and address any concerns you may have, so please do not hesitate to get in touch with us. Our team is more than willing to provide you with the information you need and help you in any way possible.

Regardless of the size of your company, we are excited at the prospect of collaborating with you. Whether you are a small startup or a large corporation, we look forward to the opportunity to work together and achieve mutual success. Our team is eager to bring our expertise and resources to the table, in order to help your business thrive and reach its full potential.

Why Choose us

1. Socks Source Factory with Advanced Manufacturing As a direct socks manufacturer, we control every step of production - from yarn sourcing to final packaging. Our state-of-the-art facility houses 50+ computerized knitting machines, enabling us to produce

5,000,000+ pairs annually with precision and efficiency.

By eliminating middlemen, we offer competitive wholesale prices without compromising quality.

Key Products:

•Custom Socks for All: Men’s socks, Women’s socks, Kids socks, Pet socks

•Seasonal & Holiday Socks: Christmas socks, Halloween socks,Valentine'sthemed socks.

•Functional Socks: Anti-slip socks,Compression socks,Bamboo cottonsocks.

2. Global Expertise in Socks Customization

Leveraging our founder’s 10+ years of international trade experience. we understand the unique needs of global clients. Whether you're a startup seeking small-batch custom socks or a retailer needing bulk wholesale orders, we provide:

•Flexible MOQs< (Starting from 500 pairs)

•100+ Design Templates & Free Sampling Service. •Eco-Friendly Options: Recycled yarns, OEKO-TEX certified dyes

3. Quality That Speaks for Itself
We pride ourselves on delivering socks that combine comfort, durability, and style.

Every pair undergoes rigorous 6-step quality control, ensuring:

•Skin-Friendly Materials: Premium cotton, moisture-wicking polyester, stretchable spandex

•Vibrant Printing & Embroidery:• Digital printing for complex patterns, 3D silicone grips for non-slip soles

4. Serving Clients Worldwide
From Europe to North America, our socks have reached 30+ countries.

We support seamless global trade with:

•Fast Turnaround: 15-25 days production time.

•Reliable Logistics. FOB, EXW, DDP terms.
